# 🛒 BuyNest E-Commerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI Project)

## 📌 Project Overview  
This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales data to uncover key business insights related to revenue, profit, customer behavior, and product performance.  

An **interactive Power BI dashboard** was built to help stakeholders track KPIs, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

The dashboard provides a **holistic view of sales performance** using dynamic visuals, filters, and drill-down capabilities.

---

## 🎯 Objectives  
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance  
- Identify high-performing products and categories  
- Understand customer purchasing behavior  
- Track monthly trends and seasonal patterns  
- Evaluate payment preferences and order distribution

## 📈 Dashboard Features  

### 📅 Sales & Profit Analysis  
- Monthly profit trends highlight growth and loss periods  
- Identification of negative profit months (May, June, October)  
- Seasonal demand patterns across the year  

### 🛍️ Category Performance  
- Clothing contributes ~58% of total quantity  
- Electronics and Furniture have smaller shares  
- Helps in inventory planning and marketing focus  

### 👥 Customer Insights  
- Top customers contribute a major portion of revenue  
- Identifies loyal and high-value customers  

### 💳 Payment Mode Analysis  
- COD is the most preferred payment method (~34%)  
- Followed by Debit Card and UPI  
- Useful for optimizing checkout experience  

### 🌍 Regional Analysis  
- State-level filtering (e.g., Bihar)  
- Enables localized performance tracking  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights  

- 📌 Clothing is the **top-performing category**  
- 📉 Some months show **negative profit**, indicating cost issues  
- 💳 COD dominates, showing customer trust or accessibility gaps  
- 👥 Revenue is concentrated among **few key customers**  
- 📊 Sales trends are **inconsistent**, indicating demand fluctuations  

---

## 💡 Recommendations  

- Increase marketing during **low-performing months**  
- Optimize pricing and cost strategies in **loss-making periods**  
- Focus on scaling **high-performing categories**  
- Encourage digital payments over COD using **discounts/offers**  
- Build loyalty programs for **repeat customers**
